The Five Pillars of Islam

Shahada

لَا إِلَٰهَ إِلَّا ٱللَّٰهُ مُحَمَّدٌ رَسُولُ ٱللَّٰهِ

Declaration of faith in the oneness of Allah and the messengership of Muhammad (peace be upon him).

Salah

الصلاة

Five daily prayers that are obligatory for all Muslims, serving as a direct connection with Allah.

Zakat

الزكاة

Annual charity giving that is obligatory for those who meet the necessary criteria of wealth.

Sawm

الصوم

Fasting during the month of Ramadan from dawn until sunset, abstaining from food, drink, and other physical needs.

Hajj

الحج

Pilgrimage to Mecca that every able Muslim must perform at least once in their lifetime.
